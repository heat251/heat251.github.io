SERVER RULES

If a rule is not explicitly stated, it can still be implicitly enforced. Use common sense.

Server admins have the final say on rule interpretation.

 

  • No RDM (Random Deathmatch). You must have proof in order to kill someone. RDMing a traitor is still RDM.
  • No revenge RDM. Report the player and accept the admin ruling.
  • You will be held responsible for calling KOS (Kill On Sight) without proof that results in RDM.
  • Do not fill out false or blank RDM reports. You will get a warning and be slain all subsequent times.
  • No traitor baiting (eg: shooting randomly, destroying testers, saying you are a traitor).
  • No ghosting; If you are dead, stay quiet if using Discord.
  • Do not call KOS (Kill On Sight) based on location or weapon.
  • Prop killing is RDM unless a prop duel is accepted.
  • No bug abuse/exploits. (eg: going out of map bounds)
  • Incendiary grenades can be thrown at others as long as they are warned and are physically able to leave the target area. 
  • You do not have to listen to anything a detective or innocent tells you to do but keep in mind it will be seen as very suspicious. However, they cannot kill you for disobeying.
